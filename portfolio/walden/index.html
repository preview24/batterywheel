Walden’s Creek

Walden’s Creek is a scenic area in Sevier County, Tennessee, located near Pigeon Forge and Wears Valley. It’s known for its peaceful countryside, mountain views, and proximity to the Great Smoky Mountains National Park. While less commercialized than nearby Gatlinburg or Pigeon Forge, it offers a more laid-back and rustic retreat.
